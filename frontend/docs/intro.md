# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook

Welcome to the **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics** interactive textbook! This comprehensive guide explores the fundamentals of embodied artificial intelligence and robotic systems.

## What You'll Learn

This textbook covers 8 core chapters on Physical AI:

1. **Introduction to Physical AI** - Fundamentals of embodied intelligence
2. **Sensors and Perception** - How robots perceive the world
3. **Kinematics and Dynamics** - Motion and forces in robotic systems
4. **Motion Planning** - Path planning and trajectory generation
5. **Control Systems** - Feedback control and stabilization
6. **Learning and Adaptation** - Machine learning for robotics
7. **Human-Robot Interaction** - Collaborative and social robotics
8. **Real-World Applications** - Industry use cases and deployments
